ABOUT

Valuing Health

Consumers are growing more concerned about the ingredients that are found in the foods they eat, and rightfully so. We recognize that gluten is a poor choice for one's health, and that it triggers symptoms that are more severe for some, over the short and long term. Our goods demonstrate that it is very possible to create delicious foods that are also nutritional, and may be enjoyed by those that suffer from Celiac disease and other disorders that call for a gluten-free diet.

Proudly Owned

Greg Whittaker, our founder and co-owner, has been gluten-free for the majority of his life, not because he is required by his health condition, but because he chooses to live a healthy lifestyle, which includes maintaining a fit and balanced diet. Greg knows firsthand how difficult it can be to find foods that are tasty and reasonably priced that are free of gluten and wheat, so he created a lovable atmosphere where people can toss away their worries concerning what is in their foods.

Giving Back

We prepare our bakery items fresh each morning, which leads to leftovers at the end of each business day. Any product that has gone unused or unpurchased is donated to local nonprofit organizations. Warm Cloud wants to promote healthy living amongst our entire community however we can, including those with special dietary needs, especially gluten-free requirements.
