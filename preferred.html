Perks of the Program

Offers

You are family to us, and we enjoy seeing you each visit. With your tenth purchase, we will reduce your final bill by ten percent. Track your visits by showing us your member card at the register. Your twenty-fifth visit is our treat, up to twenty-five dollars.

Discounts

As a preferred member, each time you see us, upon showing your member card, we will deduct five percent of your total. Stop by between the hours of 2pm and 4pm, and we will knock ten percent off of your final bill.

Birthday Benefits

As a preferred member, we want you to spend your birthday with us. On each preferred member's special day, any dessert of their choice will be served on the house.
